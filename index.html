Official Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

Welcome to Odisha Exam Prep. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This policy explains how we handle your information.

This app is a Free service. We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

1. Information Collection

To provide a personalized learning experience, we collect the following:

  • Personal Identifiers: Your name and email address (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Performance Data: Mock test scores, attempted questions, and progress history.
  • Device Information: Model, OS version, and unique device identifiers for analytics.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted industry-standard services to improve our app performance and security:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Cloud Messaging)
  • AdMob (Advertising)

3. Push Notifications

We send alerts for new mock tests and exam notifications. You can manage these permissions at any time through your Device Settings > Notifications.

4. Security Measures

We prioritize the security of your data using encrypted Firebase protocols. While we strive for 100% security, please remember that no method of electronic storage is absolute.

5. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
